BUILD NOTES:
 - The mod-lattice FOLD must use FLOOR (true modulo into the fundamental cell), NOT round —
   round clips the negative-residual half and silently drops ~half the content (incl. a word).
 - Fold anchor MUST be lattice-canonical (seed-independent), NOT the boxed position — that is
   what makes the result identical whichever word the user boxes.
 - #62 interaction is a MITIGATION, not a blocker: folding the WHOLE-image content averages
   detectWatermark per-instance bbox variance (CORE-23,25) across all instances, so the unit is
   more robust than any single seed. On HARDER fixtures (faint/missed instances) removal
   completeness is still bounded by what detectWatermark finds SOMEWHERE — a robustness caveat.
 - Residual 1px/seed basis delta (415 vs 414) leaves a sub-stroke fringe on far instances;
   fill dilation (#54) absorbs it. Optional polish: snap both boxes to one canonical integer basis.
